    How to play

    Goal: Get the lowest total score to par. You have 4 golfers; pick one tournament result per golfer. Those 4 scores add up — lower is better (e.g. −8 beats +2).

    You’ll see four columns (one per golfer), each with three face-down cards. Tap a card to reveal that round’s score. Once you flip a card for a golfer, that choice is locked — so flip wisely. Pick one card per golfer to set your final score.

    One puzzle per day — resets at midnight Central.

    *All players made the cut.

    How to play

    Objective

    Pick one historical tournament per golfer to minimize your total score to par. The score of the tournament chosen will contribute to your score. Lower is better (e.g. −5 beats +3).

    How to play

    • Each column is one golfer with three face-down score cards.
    • Flip a card to reveal that round's score to par.
    • Once flipped, that golfer's score is locked in.
    • Pick one card per golfer — lowest total wins.

    Game modes

    Best Ball uses only the four majors: Masters, PGA Championship, U.S. Open, and The Open Championship. Best Ball (Hard) uses all tournaments 2001–2026. Choose the mode on the Better Season home screen.

    *All players in the data made the cut for the tournaments shown.
