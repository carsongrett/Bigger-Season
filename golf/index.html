How to play

Objective

Pick one historical event per golfer to minimize your total score to par. The score of the event chosen will contribute to your score. Lower is better (e.g. −5 beats +3).

How to play

  • Each column is one golfer with three face-down score cards.
  • Flip a card to reveal that round's score to par.
  • Once flipped, that golfer's score is locked in.
  • Pick one card per golfer — lowest total wins.

Game modes

Best Ball uses only the four majors: Masters, PGA Championship, U.S. Open, and The Open Championship. Best Ball (Hard) uses all tournaments 2020–2025. Choose the mode on the Better Season home screen.

*All players in the data made the cut for the events shown.
